What are NFC tags?

NFC (near field communication) tags transmit information across short distance to the NFC enabled devices, e.g. your smartphones. The tags work without batteries, and often look like tokens or stickers.

TagHunt tags are made of waterproof and anti-metal high quality NFC tags, which is an excellent solution for both indoor and outdoor activities.
